    About

    The "Information technologies and management" faculty was founded in 1961 with the establishment of the "Production process automation" faculty. This faculty was created as a result of the historical necessity related to the problems of automation, management and the development and operation of the technical tools that enable them to be implemented in the fields of energy, oil-gas and petrochemicals.
