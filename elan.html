    ANNOUNCEMENT
    III INTERNATIONAL SCIENTIFIC CONFERENCE on
    RECONSTRUCTION AND RECOVERY IN POST-CONFLICT SITUATIONS
    ISCRRPCS-2023

    March 02-03, 2023, Baku, Azerbaijan
    Organized by: Azerbaijan State Oil and Industry University
    Chairman: Prof. M. Babanli
    Co-chairman: Prof. R. Aliev

    ORGANIZING COMMITTEE

    Chairwoman: L. Gardashova
    Co-chairman: T. Abdullayev
    Members:
    G. Mammadov, R. Jamalov, S. Huseynov, A. Babayev, Z. İbrahimli, N. Pashayev, S. Yusifov, I. Rustamov, S. Mammadkhanova, N. Aliyeva, R. Ismayilova, G. Aliyeva, S.Salmanov, A. Dadashova, K. Abdullaeva, S.Amirova.

    The Topics of the Conference

    1. Recovery and reconstruction and forced displacement caused by conflicts 11. Theoretical issues and models of post-conflict policies and planning.
    2. National resources, ecological problems 12. Security for households and communities, including economic security
    3. The current state and organization of the integrated use of under­ground and surface water bodies in the liberated regions of Karabakh. 13. Application of new technological approaches and advanced materials in solving environmental problems
    4. The petrochemical industry and environmental safety 14. Recovery and reconstruction of businesses and firms
    5. International community role 15. Mitigate business risk
    6. Religious and historical monuments of Karabakh and Armenian vandalism 16. Recovery and reconstruction of cultural and historical sites
    7. Historical and cultural monuments of Shusha city and their condition after the occupation 17. Education and science
    8. The current state of exploitation of mineral deposits in the liberated regions of Karabakh 18. Monitoring data mining (Big Data) modelling and simulations
    9. The long-term impact of conflict 19. Economic development of regions
    10. Economic policy priorities 20. Prospects for the development of energy security and renewable energy sources.

    Papers describing original work (up to 8 pages), but not limited to these research areas, are invited. The conference paper will be published in conference proceedings (selected papers in journals indexed in Scopus, Web of Science, Elsevier).
